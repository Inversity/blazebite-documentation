## üöÄ Step-by-Step Setup Instructions

### Step 1: Power On the Tablet

1. **Plug in the tablet** to power using the provided charger
2. **Press and hold** the power button (usually on the side or top edge) for 2-3 seconds
3. **Wait** for the tablet to boot up (this may take 30-60 seconds)
4. You should see the Android home screen or lock screen

‚úÖ **Success Check:** Tablet displays the home screen or lock screen

---

### Step 2: Connect to WiFi

1. **Swipe down** from the top of the screen to open Quick Settings
2. **Tap** the WiFi icon to view available networks
3. **Select** your venue's WiFi network from the list
4. **Enter** your WiFi password carefully
5. **Tap** "Connect"
6. **Wait** for the WiFi icon to show a connection (usually shows signal bars)

üí° **Tip:** If you mistype the password, tap "Forget Network" and try connecting again.

‚úÖ **Success Check:** WiFi icon in the status bar shows connected with signal bars

‚ö†Ô∏è **Troubleshooting WiFi:**
- **Network not appearing?** Make sure your WiFi router is powered on and broadcasting
- **Wrong password error?** Double-check for typos, especially with similar characters (0 vs O, 1 vs l)
- **Connection drops?** Position tablet closer to the WiFi router and try again

---

### Step 3: Open the BlazeBite Kitchen App

1. **Locate** the BlazeBite Kitchen app icon on the tablet home screen
   - Look for the BlazeBite logo (orange/red flame icon)
   - If not visible, check the app drawer (swipe up from bottom of screen)

2. **Tap** the BlazeBite Kitchen app icon to launch it

3. **Wait** for the app to load (first launch may take 10-15 seconds)

‚úÖ **Success Check:** The BlazeBite Kitchen login screen appears

‚ö†Ô∏è **App not installed?** Contact BlazeBite support - the app should be pre-installed via Esper MDM.

---

### Step 4: Log In to the Kitchen App

1. **Enter your email address** in the Email field
   - This is the same email you use for the BlazeBite Portal

2. **Enter your password** in the Password field
   - This is the same password you use for the BlazeBite Portal

3. **Tap** the "Log In" button

4. **Wait** for authentication (usually 2-5 seconds)

‚úÖ **Success Check:** You see the Kitchen app main screen with "Orders" at the top

‚ö†Ô∏è **Login Troubleshooting:**
- **"Invalid credentials" error?** Double-check your email and password
- **"Network error"?** Verify your WiFi connection is working
- **"Account not found"?** Contact support to verify your account is set up correctly

## üñ®Ô∏è Connecting Your Bluetooth Printer

### Part A: Pair the Printer (One-Time Setup)

First, you need to pair the printer with the tablet using Android's Bluetooth settings through Esper:

1. **Power on your Star TSP100 printer**
   - Connect printer to power outlet
   - Press the power button on the back
   - Wait for the printer to initialize (lights will stop flashing)

2. **Open Esper Settings on the tablet**
   - Swipe down from the top of the screen with two fingers
   - Tap the gear icon (‚öôÔ∏è) to open Esper Settings
   - Or find "Esper Settings" in the app drawer

3. **Navigate to Bluetooth settings**
   - Tap "Connected Devices" or "Bluetooth"
   - Make sure Bluetooth is turned **ON** (toggle switch should be blue/green)

4. **Put printer in pairing mode**
   - Your TSP100 should appear in the "Available Devices" list automatically
   - If not, hold the printer's feed button for 3 seconds
   - Look for device names like "TSP100", "Star TSP100", or similar

5. **Pair the printer**
   - Tap on your printer's name in the Available Devices list
   - If prompted for a PIN, try: `0000`, `1234`, or `1111`
   - Wait for "Paired" or "Connected" status

‚úÖ **Success Check:** Printer shows as "Paired" or "Connected" in Bluetooth settings

‚ö†Ô∏è **Pairing Troubleshooting:**
- **Printer not appearing?** Make sure it's powered on and within 10 feet of the tablet
- **Pairing failed?** Turn Bluetooth off and on again, then retry
- **Wrong PIN?** Try common PINs: 0000, 1234, 1111, 9999

---

### Part B: Connect Printer in Kitchen App

After pairing in Esper Settings, you need to connect the printer within the Kitchen app:

1. **Open the Kitchen app** (if not already open)

2. **Open the hamburger menu** (‚ò∞) and tap **Settings**

3. **Tap "Printer Settings"** or "Hardware"

4. **Tap "Connect Printer"** or "Select Printer"

5. **Choose your printer** from the list
   - You should see your TSP100 printer listed
   - Tap on the printer name to select it

6. **Wait for connection confirmation**
   - The app will test the connection
   - You should see "Printer Connected" or a green checkmark

‚úÖ **Success Check:** Settings screen shows printer status as "Connected" with printer name

‚ö†Ô∏è **App Connection Troubleshooting:**
- **Printer not listed?** Go back and verify the printer is paired in Esper Settings
- **Connection failed?** Ensure printer is powered on and has paper loaded
- **"Printer offline"?** Turn the printer off and on again, then reconnect

---

### Step 5: Test Print Your First Order Ticket

Now let's verify everything is working correctly:

1. **Load thermal paper** in the printer
   - Open the printer cover
   - Insert paper roll with paper feeding from the bottom
   - Pull a few inches of paper through the output slot
   - Close the cover until it clicks

2. **In the Kitchen app, go to Settings**

3. **Find "Test Print" or "Print Test Page"**

4. **Tap to print a test ticket**

5. **Verify the printout**
   - Check that text is clear and readable
   - Ensure all information printed completely
   - Verify paper cuts cleanly (if auto-cut is enabled)

‚úÖ **Success Check:** Test ticket prints clearly with all text visible

‚ö†Ô∏è **Print Troubleshooting:**
- **Nothing prints?** Check that printer is connected in Kitchen app, not just paired in Bluetooth
- **Blank paper feeds?** Paper may be loaded backwards - check that thermal side faces the print head
- **Garbled text?** Reconnect printer in Kitchen app Settings
- **Paper jam?** Open printer cover, remove paper, check for obstructions, reload paper

üí° **Tip:** The thermal side of the paper is usually slightly shinier or you can test by scratching it with your fingernail - it will leave a dark mark.

## üîß Quick Troubleshooting Guide

### Tablet Issues

**Tablet won't turn on**
- Ensure it's plugged into power
- Hold power button for 10 seconds
- Try a different power outlet

**WiFi keeps disconnecting**
- Move tablet closer to router
- Restart the tablet
- Check if other devices have WiFi issues (might be router problem)

**App is slow or frozen**
- Force close the app: Settings > Apps > BlazeBite Kitchen > Force Stop
- Reopen the app
- If problem persists, restart the tablet

---

### Printer Issues

**Printer won't connect**
1. Verify printer is powered on
2. Check Bluetooth pairing in Esper Settings (Settings > Bluetooth)
3. If paired but not connecting, forget the device and re-pair
4. Reconnect in Kitchen app Settings

**Printer connected but won't print**
- Check paper is loaded correctly (thermal side down)
- Verify printer has power and is online
- Try test print from printer hardware (hold feed button)
- Reconnect printer in Kitchen app

**Faded or blank prints**
- Replace thermal paper (old paper loses quality)
- Check paper is loaded with thermal side facing print head
- Clean print head with alcohol wipe (printer powered off)

**Paper jams**
- Power off printer
- Open cover and remove jammed paper
- Check for torn paper pieces or obstructions
- Reload paper properly
- Close cover and power on

---

### Order & App Issues

**Orders not showing up**
- Verify WiFi connection (check signal strength)
- Confirm menu is published and active in Portal
- Check Portal settings for order routing
- Try logging out and back in

**Can't mark order as ready**
- Ensure you have a stable internet connection
- Check if order is already marked complete
- Try force closing and reopening the app

**App crashes on startup**
- Clear app cache: Esper Settings > Apps > BlazeBite Kitchen > Clear Cache
- Restart tablet
- If still failing, contact support (may need app reinstall)

---

### Network Issues

**"Network Error" messages**
- Check WiFi connection (swipe down and verify WiFi is connected)
- Test internet by opening a web browser
- Restart WiFi router if available
- Contact venue IT support
